Collaboration

Leaflet is a web based collaboration application that is designed to help people come together.

From student level note taking collaboration to large scale corporate mettings, Leaflet is here. Document with ease for yourself or invite others and work together. With a simplicity you can start taking notes for what ever the occasion may be.

Flesh & Bone

  • Back to the basics: Simple and to the point. Quickly create your notes and share them public or with the user group. Wait for collaboration!
  • Delegate: Vote the notes you find relevant and down vote the ones that are not. As the user you decide what is important.
  • Accuracy: With Leaflet's integrated templates you can take notes for an task you may have.
